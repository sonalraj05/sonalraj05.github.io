CBHS Robotics

Vex Robotics is a competition for high school students which invloves designing, building, coding, and driving robots. Each year a new game is released with a certain objective. Two teams, with two robots per team compete to score the highest score and win. Chirstchurch Boys High has been running a Vex Robotics program for 5 years and we have been extremely successful. We have won numerous awards in design and driver skills. In 2019, the only CBHS team at the time won the national championships in Auckland and qualified for the world competition in the US. The program is beigger than ever before and meets every day afterschool in f10.
